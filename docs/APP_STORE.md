### Description (4000 chars max)

Drop blocks. Clear lines. Chase the high score.

Block Royale is a block puzzle game stripped down to what matters: sharp strategy, satisfying clears, and one-more-game addiction. No timers. No lives. No waiting. Just you and the 8x8 grid.

THREE PIECES AT A TIME

Each round deals you three shapes. Drag them onto the board, fill rows or columns to clear them, and keep the grid alive as long as you can. Simple to learn. Brutal to master.

COMBOS THAT HIT DIFFERENT

Clear multiple lines in one move and watch the combo counter climb. Neon effects light up the board. Every big clear feels earned.

DESIGNED FOR SHORT SESSIONS

Waiting for coffee? Got five minutes? Block Royale fits into the gaps. Pick up, play, put down. Your high score is always waiting when you come back.

DARK NEON AESTHETIC

No pastel cartoon nonsense. Block Royale runs a dark arcade look with neon highlights, 3D candy-glass blocks, and particle effects on every clear. It looks as good as it plays.

FEATURES

- 8x8 grid with drag-and-drop controls
- 7 vibrant block colours with rim-lit 3D styling
- Line clear animations with neon glow and particles
- Combo scoring system with escalating multipliers
- Local leaderboard tracking your top 10 scores
- Dark neon theme
- Synthesised sound effects
- Works offline -- fully playable without a connection
- No account required. No sign-up. Just play.

Built by a one-person studio. No data harvesting. No dark patterns. Just a clean puzzle game.
