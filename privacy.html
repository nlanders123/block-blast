Privacy Policy

Block Royale — Effective April 2026

Overview

Block Royale is a block puzzle game built by Neil Landers. Your privacy is straightforward: the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

Block Royale does not collect any personal information. There are no user accounts, no sign-up, and no login. The app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

On-Device Storage

The app stores your high scores and game settings locally on your device using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Block Royale does not integrate any third-party services or SDKs that collect data.

Children's Privacy

Because the app does not collect any data, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
